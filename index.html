What I'm about

....and what I have to offer

My name is Richard Evan Baker, and I am a self educated web developer and designer. I currently reside in San Francisco and it's a blessing to be in the heart of tech. Ever since I was very young I have had a strong interest in computers and how they work. I have never had the opportunity to pursue my love until now, and thus far it has been a great experience.

I really think it takes passion to reach programming nirvana. The intricate design of a simple program involves intuition and intelligence; that is only just the surface, and that is where I come in. I am absolutely obsessed with learning new web technologies, languages, programming, and new things overall. I love being in an environment where I can further my skills and share the talents and ingenuity I have to offer.

My portfolio is always growing so feel free to check out my work or contact me.
